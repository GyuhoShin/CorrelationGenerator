About

An interactive tool for exploring correlations between socioeconomic variables across the 50 U.S. states and the District of Columbia. Pick any pair of variables to see a scatter plot with an OLS regression line and the corresponding statistics.

Source

U.S. Census Bureau, 2023 American Community Survey (ACS) 1-Year Estimates.
